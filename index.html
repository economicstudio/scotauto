Scotauto: Steem Scot Claim Automation

Scotauto claims all scot tokens (and steem reward) every 1 hour (frequency may be subject to change).
General instruction: https://steemit.com/@blockchainstudio/scotauto

Authorize scotauto

(Using Keychain)

Alternatively, click on https://api.steemconnect.com/authorize/scotauto (Using Steemconnect)

Note: If you fail to authorize even with a valid key, it's highly likely that you've already authorized your posting right to too many dapps (max is 40). (See details here if you're interested: [suggestion] Increase max number of dapps that a user can use]

Anyway, in that case, to use @scotauto, you should revoke one of apps that you no longer use. Review dapps by visiting https://steemd.com/@[yoursteemitid] and remove unused one. For instance, knacksteem was shut down while ago (even without a proper notice.) You can remove knacksteem here: https://beta.steemconnect.com/revoke/knacksteem.app (change the last app name to the app you wanna remove)

To use scotauto

After authorization, write a reply to https://steemit.com/@blockchainstudio/scotauto
Currently, there is no automatic enrollment process, so please be patient.

To stop using scotauto (revoke scotauto)



Alternatively, click on https://api.steemconnect.com/revoke/scotauto (Using Steemconnect)
